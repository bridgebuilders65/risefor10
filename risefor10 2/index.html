How it started

On May 9, 2026 — Didi Krishna's 65th birthday — over 70 healthcare providers across 14 countries answered a call to serve, whether it meant waiving a fee, providing a free surgery, extra time and care.

Risefor10 is a vision to take this even further: providers everywhere will aim to give 10% of their time, their talent, their treasure to build a kinder, healthier world.
“Reverence is the root, and service is the fruit. Once you grow in reverence, you realize that the purpose of life is service. Then you become a giver. Love in action is compassion.”
— Didi Krishna
